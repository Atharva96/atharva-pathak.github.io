Monitoring & Control

Built comprehensive monitoring, alerting, and data quality frameworks across all analytics pipelines and dashboards — ensuring operational reliability and sustained trust in enterprise reporting.

MonitoringData QualityAlertingADFPower AutomateSQL

1 Business Need

With multiple dashboards and pipelines in production, failures and data quality issues were often discovered only when stakeholders reported incorrect numbers. The organization needed proactive monitoring and systematic data quality checks.

2 What I Built

  • Pipeline health monitoring dashboard tracking run times, success rates, and failure patterns
  • Automated alerting for pipeline failures, data quality violations, and refresh anomalies
  • Data quality validation rules at each transformation stage
  • Refresh log parsing and status reporting integrated with WhatsApp notifications
  • Incident tracking and resolution workflow for rapid response

3 Tech Stack

  • Monitoring: ADF pipeline activity logs and custom health dashboards
  • Alerting: Power Automate flows with conditional routing
  • Data Quality: SQL-based validation rules and check procedures
  • Reporting: Power BI operational dashboards for pipeline visibility
  • Communication: Integrated WhatsApp and email notifications

4 Impact & Outcomes

  • Reduced issue detection time from hours to minutes through proactive alerting
  • Improved data trust — stakeholders confident in the numbers they see
  • Quality gates prevented bad data from reaching production dashboards
  • Operational visibility enabled capacity planning and optimization
  • Reduced risk of incorrect decisions based on stale or erroneous data
