Customer Behavior Analysis

Python-based deep dive into Adidas Germany ecommerce data

Used Python to analyze customer behavior across the Adidas Germany ecommerce platform — purchase frequency, browsing patterns, cart abandonment, repeat purchase behavior, and product affinity. The analysis segmented customers into actionable cohorts and identified key drivers of churn and retention.

What I Analyzed

  • Purchase frequency and recency patterns across customer segments
  • Cart abandonment rates and drop-off points in the funnel
  • Repeat purchase behavior and product category affinity
  • Customer lifecycle stages — new, active, at-risk, dormant

Outcomes

  • 10% improvement in customer retention through targeted interventions
  • 5% increase in customer lifetime value via upsell/cross-sell recommendations
  • 15% reduction in customer acquisition costs by focusing on high-value segments
  • Identified top churn signals enabling proactive retention campaigns
