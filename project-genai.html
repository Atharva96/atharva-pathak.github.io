Content Intelligence

Generative AI — Automating audio/video content processing

The executive education platform had hours of faculty-recorded audio and video content that was completely unsearchable and unindexed. Every piece of content required manual transcription before it could be cataloged, repurposed, or made discoverable. I built a Speech-to-Text engine using Generative AI that automated this entirely.

What I Built

  • Speech-to-Text engine for automated transcription of faculty lectures and webinars
  • Processing pipeline that takes raw audio/video and outputs structured, searchable text
  • Integration with the content library for automatic indexing and tagging

Business Impact

  • 25% reduction in content processing time — from manual hours to automated minutes
  • Enabled searchable content libraries for the first time — students could search inside lectures
  • Set the automation standard that was adopted across other content workflows in the organization

User Understanding

NLP — Turning unstructured reviews into actionable personas

Student reviews were scattered across Google, app stores, social media, and internal feedback forms — all unstructured text with no systematic way to extract insights. I engineered an NLP model that processed reviews from multiple platforms, generated sentiment scores, and contributed to building valuable user personas that product and marketing teams actually used for decision-making.

What I Built

  • NLP pipeline ingesting reviews from Google, app stores, social media, and internal forms
  • Sentiment scoring model classifying feedback as positive, negative, or neutral with topic extraction
  • User persona framework built from sentiment patterns — used by product and marketing teams

How It Was Used

  • Product team used personas to prioritize feature development and course improvements
  • Marketing team used sentiment data to craft targeted messaging and address pain points
  • Leadership used aggregated sentiment trends in quarterly business reviews

Course Recommendation & Price Prediction

Machine Learning — Right course, right person, right price

Two connected ML models that served the same business goal: getting the right course to the right learner at the right price. The recommendation engine matched learners with courses based on their behavior, goals, and enrollment patterns. The price prediction model optimized pricing based on market dynamics, competitor benchmarks, and demand signals.

Course Recommendation

  • ML model analyzing learner behavior, browsing history, and enrollment patterns
  • Personalized course suggestions surfaced to prospective students
  • Improved course discovery for learners who didn't know what to pick

Price Prediction

  • Predictive model factoring in market dynamics, competitor pricing, and demand signals
  • Optimized pricing for new and existing courses to maximize enrollment and revenue
  • Informed go-to-market pricing strategy for program launches

The executive education platform faced three core challenges: hours of content locked in audio/video with no searchability, student feedback scattered across platforms with no way to extract insights, and course matching based on generic logic rather than learner behavior.
