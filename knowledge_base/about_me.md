# About Me

Atharva Pathak is a senior analytics and business intelligence professional with experience across enterprise BI, data engineering, product analytics, customer analytics, and AI-driven automation. He currently works as **Deputy Manager – Business Intelligence at Polycab India Limited**, where he owns CXO-level decision systems supporting Sales and Marketing functions. His work spans executive dashboarding, Azure-based data engineering, reporting automation, and AI-enabled self-service analytics.

He specializes in converting large-scale business data into actionable insights through a combination of **Power BI, SQL, Python, Azure Data Engineering, and AI-powered decision support**. Over the course of his career, he has worked on enterprise-grade initiatives such as Pan-India Sales Incentive dashboards, Marketing ROI reporting platforms, natural-language-to-SQL copilots, and automation solutions that improve leadership visibility and reduce manual reporting effort.

Atharva brings a rare combination of **business understanding, technical depth, and ownership mindset**. He is particularly effective in environments that demand scalable reporting systems, cross-functional stakeholder management, performance optimization, and measurable business impact. His background includes building data pipelines with Azure Data Factory, Databricks, Data Lake, and Synapse Analytics, while also designing dashboards and analytical products used by senior leadership for strategic decision-making.

Before Polycab, he worked across analytics and product-focused roles at **Times Group** and **eClerx**, where he developed Generative AI solutions, NLP-based sentiment models, web-scraping intelligence frameworks, customer behavior analytics, and decision-support dashboards. Earlier in his career, he also built backend applications and workflow systems as a **Django Developer**, giving him a strong engineering foundation that continues to support his work in analytics automation and AI integration.
